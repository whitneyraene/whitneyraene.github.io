Whitney Raene Rodriguez was born on August 10th, 1989 in Whittier, California. Whitney grew up in La Puente, ca where she attend Fairgrove Academy(K-8th) which is where she got her start in acting. Her first performance was in Fairgrove’s USO show, a musical revue. From then on she auditioned and got cast in every spring musical. She performed in shows such as Bye, Bye, Birdie; The Music Man; and Annie.

After that, she decided to continue on with acting by auditioning for the Los Angeles County High School for the Arts. She was accepted and enrolled for fall 2003. Her four years of high school at LACHSA, were spent training her in voice, speech, movement, and acting. She got trained in Suzuki and viewpoints and in the International Phonetic Alphabet, something students at a regular high school would not get the opportunity to learn. She was able to perform in many different venues including the Alex Theater in Glendale, Ivar Theater, Barnsdall Theater and Shakespeare Center LA.

Yet again Whitney choose the artistic route, when applying to College she knew she wanted to major in acting. She got accepted in the California Institute for the Arts, Calarts became her new home. Now after four years of rigorous training in voice, speech, singing, puppeting, movement, acting and much more she is graduating and earning her Bachelors of Fine Arts Degree in Theater.
